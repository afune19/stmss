GUIDANCE & COUNSELLING

Empowering Hearts, Minds, and Souls

Our Mission

At ST. Mary's School, our Guidance & Counselling team is dedicated to fostering a supportive environment where students can thrive emotionally, academically, and spiritually. Through compassionate listening and faith-driven guidance, we empower every student to discover their purpose, overcome challenges, and embrace a future full of possibilities.
Your journey of growth and self-discovery starts here.

What We Offer

- Personal Counselling: Confidential support for students dealing with personal challenges.
- Career Guidance: Assistance in choosing the right academic and career path based on strengths and interests.
- Spiritual Growth: Opportunities for spiritual development through prayer sessions and retreats.

How to Access Support

Students can book one-on-one sessions with our counsellors or participate in group workshops. Parents are also welcome to seek guidance on how to best support their children.
