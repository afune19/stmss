GUIDANCE & COUNSELLING

Empowering Hearts, Minds, and Souls

Our Mission

The mission of the Guidance and Counselling Department is to provide a supportive and nurturing environment where students can receive personalized assistance to thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. We are committed to helping students develop essential life skills, build resilience, and make informed decisions that promote their well-being and success. Your journey of growth and self-discovery starts here.

What We Offer

We offer a range of services designed to support students in overcoming challenges and achieving their full potential. Our services include one-on-one counselling sessions, group counselling, career guidance, conflict resolution, stress management, and support for personal issues such as family concerns, self-esteem, and peer relationships. We also provide workshops on life skills, goal setting, and academic planning.

How to Access Support

Students can access support by scheduling an appointment with the Guidance and Counselling team through the school’s designated system, or by speaking directly to a counsellor. We encourage students, parents, and staff to reach out whenever they feel in need of guidance or assistance. Our services are confidential, and we are here to help every student feel heard, valued, and supported.
