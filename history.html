History of the School

St. Maryâ€™s Primary School was established in 1991 by Kilimanjaro Contractors under the guidance of the late Fr. Linno Zicco (Comboni Missionary) with funds donated by the Christians of Piacenza, Italy. The original construction included eight classrooms, a home science room, a library, three offices, a waiting room, and ten pit latrines.

The school became operational on January 7, 1992, with 70 pupils and three staff members: Sr. Silvina Akidi (Headmistress), Mrs. Mary Wambui Muchunga (Deputy Head Teacher), and Sr. Lydia Anyango (Teacher). On April 12, 1992, it was officially opened by Rt. Rev. Colin Davies, Bishop of Ngongâ€™ Diocese. That same year, additional funds from Piacenza facilitated the construction of two classrooms for Art and Craft.

From 1996 to 2014, the school undertook various development projects without external donations. These included building eight additional classrooms, a basketball pitch, three garages, a kitchen, 12 flush toilets, and a paved path. The school also acquired three buses of varying capacities.

In May 2012, a Nursery Section was established with 15 children. By December 28, 2012, Sr. Pamela A. Agunda succeeded Sr. Silvina Akidi as Headmistress. By 2013, the school had over 450 pupils in primary and 38 in nursery.

The school celebrated its Silver Jubilee on May 4, 2018, with 784 learners in primary and 256 in nursery. Currently, the school population remains above 700. St. Maryâ€™s is recognized for its holistic education that nurtures students academically, socially, morally, and spiritually.

The school acknowledges the contributions of the late Bishop Emeritus Colin Davies, the Comboni Missionaries, the Evangelizing Sisters of Mary, staff, parents, benefactors, and Bishop John Oballa Owaa for their encouragement and support.

ST. MARY'S VISION

VISION

An academic center of excellence inspired by the Good News of Christ, to offer a holistic education that embraces every child as a unique creation of God.

ST. MARY'S MISSION

Mission Statement

St. Mary's Primary School is an academic center of excellence offering early childhood and primary level education by means of sound curricular and co-curricular programs, focused on the development and education of the whole child - spiritually, morally, intellectually, socially, emotionally, and physically.

ST. MARY'S MOTTO

Motto

Discipline, Knowledge, and Wisdom.

Core Values

  • The school articulates and manifests a Catholic and Christ-centered mission.
  • The school and the community embrace each child as a unique creation of God.
  • Respect for diversity and individuality: culture, religion, ethnicity, race, socio-economic, and academic ability of each child.
  • Quality educational programs that excel academically and form God-fearing future leaders.
  • Formation of God-fearing future leaders.
  • Service that models and witnesses a living Catholic/Christian values and lifestyles.
