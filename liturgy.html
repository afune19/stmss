Morning Prayer Guide

Lord,
As we begin this day, we offer You our thoughts, words, and actions. Guide us to make choices that honor You and bring kindness to others. Bless our learning, protect our school community, and keep us safe in Your care. Amen.

The Rosary
  • Opening Prayer – The Sign of the Cross and the Apostles' Creed.
  • Decades – Each decade involves the "Our Father," ten "Hail Marys," and a "Glory Be," reflecting on different Mysteries of the Rosary (Joyful, Sorrowful, Glorious, or Luminous).
  • Closing Prayer – The Hail Holy Queen and concluding with the Sign of the Cross.
Liturgy of the Hours

Morning Prayer (Lauds) – Offered in the morning to praise God for the day.

Daytime Prayer (Terce, Sext, None) – Short prayers during the day at around 9 a.m., noon, and 3 p.m. to sanctify the day.

Evening Prayer (Vespers) – Offered in the evening, thanking God for the day’s blessings.

Night Prayer (Compline) – Before going to bed, offering thanks for the day and seeking God’s protection through the night.

Adoration Prayers

O Lord, our God, We adore You and praise You, We glorify You for Your greatness, For You are our Creator, Redeemer, and Savior. We stand in awe before Your majesty, And offer You all the honor due to Your Holy Name. Fill our hearts with Your love and peace, So that we may serve You with a pure heart. Amen.

Traditional Catholic Prayers

Hail Mary Prayer

Hail Mary, full of grace, The Lord is with thee; Blessed art thou among women, And blessed is the fruit of thy womb, Jesus. Holy Mary, Mother of God, Pray for us sinners, Now and at the hour of our death. Amen.
