Welcome to St. Mary's Pre-school

Join us at St. Mary's Pre-school, where we nurture curiosity, creativity, and a love for learning in every child.

A Message from Our Head

It is a joy to lead a place where curiosity, creativity, and a love for learning are at the heart of everything we do. Our focus is on providing a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where young minds can explore, grow, and develop essential skills that lay the foundation for future success. With a dedicated team of educators, we create experiences that foster emotional, social, and cognitive development in each child. Thank you for entrusting us with your child’s early learning journey, and we look forward to partnering with you in shaping their bright future.

- Sr. Salome Achieng, Head of St. Mary's Pre-school
